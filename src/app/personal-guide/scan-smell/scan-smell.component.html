What is iPerfume Machine ?

iPerfume is an innovative fragrance designing machine integrated with our web application. It allows users to design his scent with his choices and experience scents directly by scanning a QR code, providing a seamless and interactive way to discover new perfumes.

How it works ?

Approach the iPerfume machine and scan the provided QR code using the scanner on the machine.
