Who We Are

With over 15 years of expertise in the world of perfumery, Kawkaba Fragrances was born from a passion for crafting exceptional scents. Launched online in 2021, we set out to redefine the fragrance experience, blending artistry with innovation. In 2025, we proudly opened our first store in Arish, marking the beginning of our expansion across Egypt to bring our scents closer to perfume lovers everywhere.

We believe that choosing a fragrance is a deeply personal journey, which is why we have seamlessly integrated technology into the world of perfumery, making it easier than ever to find the perfect scent. At Kawkaba, we don’t just create perfumes—we craft experiences, ensuring every bottle reflects elegance, quality, and a touch of individuality.

our mission

redefine luxury perfumery by offering unique, high-quality fragrances that tell a story of artistry and passion. We are committed to sourcing the finest ingredients, ensuring that every scent is a masterpiece of craftsmanship. Through continuous innovation and a dedication to excellence, we aim to provide our clients with personalized, memorable, and unparalleled fragrance experiences.

our vision

At Kawkaba Fragrances, our vision is to set new benchmarks in luxury perfumery, becoming a symbol of prestige and refinement worldwide. We aspire to create an elite fragrance house that not only caters to sophisticated tastes but also establishes an emotional connection with those who wear our scents. With a commitment to quality, creativity, and exclusivity, we strive to shape the future of perfumery, one exquisite fragrance at a time.

Our strength

At Kawkaba Fragrances, our strength lies in our commitment to premium quality ingredients, expert craftsmanship, and personalized scents that cater to individual preferences. With a customer-centric approach, we ensure an exceptional experience by blending innovation with tradition, using cutting-edge technology while staying true to the heritage of fine perfumery. Our dedication to sustainability and ethical practices drives us to use eco-friendly materials and recyclable packaging, ensuring that luxury and responsibility go hand in hand.
